A bookmarklet is a script that stays on the Bookmarks Bar waiting to be clicked to execute. To use a bookmarklet: 1. Drag and drop the link to the Bookmarks Bar. 1. Navigate to the page it's designed to run on. 1. Click it to execute. Bookmarklets tend to rot over time if the owner changes the website, please [report to me]({{ site.baseurl }}/contact) if it's broken.
